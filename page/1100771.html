aberdeen 1800–2000: a new history

Authors: w._hamish_fraser

Publish Year: 2000

Publisher: birlinn_ltd

Rating: 0.0

Description: this book provides an understanding of the huge changes that have taken place in aberdeens economic and social structure over the past 200 years from the age of textiles to the age of oil it analyzes changes in work patterns housing education economy social welfare religion local government leisure and culture and discusses the effects of national and international market forces periods of instability and high growth and political struggles it features many of the people who played an important part in this period of aberdeens history
