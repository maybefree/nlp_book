Authors: barbara_gowdy

Publish Year: 1997

Description: barbara gowdys outrageous hilarious disturbing and compassionate novel is about the canary family their immoderate passions and eccentricities and their secret lives and histories the deepest secret of all is harbored in the silence of the youngest daughter joan who doesnt grow who doesnt speak but who can play the piano like mozart though shes never had a lesson joan is a mystery and in the novels stunning climax her family comes to understand that each of them is a mystery as marvelous as joan as irreducible as the mystery of life itself in its compassionate investigation of moral truths and its bold embrace of the fractured nature of every one of its characters mister sandman attains the heightened quality of a modernday parable
