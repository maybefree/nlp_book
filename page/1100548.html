Description: weeks in this country is a collection of poems concerned with place from turkey to rural bohemia to cape cod griffith uses the rich imagery of her travels to evoke both the exotic and the personal these are poems of observation and poems of meditation ultimately they speak of the longing for connectionamong individuals between cultures and across history
