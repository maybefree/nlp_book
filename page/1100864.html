transforming higher education: views from leaders around the world

Authors: madeleine_f._green

Publish Year: 1997

Publisher: praeger

Rating: 0.0

Description: it is important for those who will lead higher education into the next century to understand the forces currently transforming postsecondary institutions worldwide in this new book editor madeleine f green identifies many of these influences including demands for greater access to higher education funding problems the conflicting requirements of various constituencies and pressures to contribute to a countrys economic and social development many of the main actors or groups that are driving change in higher education also are identified including the government the public faculty members governing boards students and institutional leaders current theory about the nature of leadership is reviewed and the various forms of leadership currently in place at higher education institutions around the world also are described
